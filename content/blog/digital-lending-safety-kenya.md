Fake lending apps steal data and PINs. Real lenders have verifiable processes. Learn the difference before you install anything.

## Never share your M-Pesa PIN

No legitimate lender asks for your PIN, not even "for verification." STK push lets you approve payments yourself on the phone.

## Check company identity

Look for:

- Registered business name matching marketing
- Working support email and in-app help
- Clear terms and privacy pages
- Fees on screen before you pay

HarakaCash publishes [terms](/terms) and [privacy](/privacy) on the site.

## Upfront cash to "release" loans

If someone asks you to send money to a personal number to unlock a bigger loan, it is fraud. Walk away.

## Clone apps

Download from official links or trusted app stores. Check developer name and reviews mentioning support quality, not just star bots.

## ID photo safety

Send ID only inside the official application flow. Do not WhatsApp photos to strangers promising fast approval.

## CRB and licensing

Digital lenders operate under Kenyan financial rules. If an app hides its legal name, ask why.

## Report suspicious contact

Save screenshots, phone numbers, and chat logs. Report to support and authorities when impersonators use a brand name.
