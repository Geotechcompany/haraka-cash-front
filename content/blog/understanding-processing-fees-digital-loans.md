Processing fee is the line item borrowers ask about most. It is not hidden interest. It is the charge for moving your file through verification and payout.

## When you pay it

On HarakaCash you pay the processing fee after you accept the offer and before CRB review and M-Pesa disbursement. If you decline the offer, you do not pay it.

## What it funds

The fee covers operational steps: identity checks, bureau pull, fraud screening, and payment rail costs to send money to M-Pesa. It is separate from interest on the principal.

## Compare total cost, not one line

Some apps advertise low interest then add fees later. Always compare:

- Principal you receive
- Interest over the term
- Processing fee
- Total due on maturity date

HarakaCash puts those on one offer screen. Screenshot it for your records.

## VAT and receipts

Keep the M-Pesa confirmation for the processing fee payment. If support asks for proof, you already have it.

## Late fees are different

Processing fee is upfront. Late fees only apply if you miss the due date. They are listed in terms, not on the initial offer.

## Borrow less if the fee stretches you

If the fee plus repayment leaves you short for rent, reduce the requested amount and re-quote. The app recalculates instantly.
