Your first digital loan sets the tone for limits and pricing later. Slow down once, save money many times.

## Borrow for a named expense

"Extra cash" is not a plan. Name the bill: school ID, rent top-up, supplier invoice. That keeps the amount honest.

## Start below the maximum

You might qualify for KES 20,000. If you need KES 8,000, take KES 8,000. Smaller loans are easier to repay and build trust.

## Read every offer line

Principal, interest, processing fee, total due, due date. If any line is missing, do not proceed.

## Schedule repayment immediately

Add due date to calendar before you spend the disbursement. First-time borrowers who do this rarely pay late fees.

## Keep the M-Pesa message

Disbursement SMS is proof of receipt. Screenshot the offer too.

## Do not test multiple apps the same day

One application gives you a clear yes or no. Scatter-gun applications hurt bureau profiles.

## Ask support when confused

In-app support beats guessing. Wrong guesses become wrong payments.
