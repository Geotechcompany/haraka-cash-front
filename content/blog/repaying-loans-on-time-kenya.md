On-time repayment is the cheapest credit upgrade available. No fee beats it.

## Know your exact due date

HarakaCash one-month products have a single maturity date on the offer. Put it in your phone calendar with two alerts: seven days before and one day before.

## Keep the amount ready early

Move repayment cash to M-Pesa before the due date. Do not wait for a last-minute Kopokopo delay on pay day.

## Pay from the same line when possible

Use the M-Pesa number on your loan profile. Some payment flows match on phone digits.

## If salary is late

Contact support before the due date. Silence is what triggers late fees. You may get a short extension when payroll slips, not when you simply forgot.

## Partial payments

Some lenders accept partial pay-downs. Ask support if you are short. Half paid on time beats full amount three days late.

## After you repay

Check your dashboard. Confirm status shows settled. Screenshot it. Limits update after settlement posts.

## Late repayment cost

Late fees apply. CRB reporting may follow. Your next approval could be smaller or more expensive. That is the real price, not just the fee line.

Build the habit on your first loan. [Borrow only what you can repay](/apply) and schedule the date the same day you accept.
