Salary advance is not free money. It is pay day moved forward. Use it when delay costs more than fees.

## Clear yes situations

- Landlord penalty after the 5th and salary lands the 7th
- School payment deadline this week
- NHIF or SHIF arrears blocking care
- Travel for urgent family matter with fixed ticket price today

## Clear no situations

- Discretionary shopping because deals end tonight
- Lending to a friend who "will pay you on salary"
- Covering a recurring gap every single month without cutting expenses

If you need an advance every cycle, the problem is structural. Fix budget or income, do not stack advances.

## Fee math in one minute

Compare processing fee plus interest to the penalty or loss you avoid. If landlord fine is KES 2,000 and advance total cost is KES 1,200, advance wins. If there is no penalty for waiting five days, wait.

## Tell the truth in the form

Salary advances work best when employer and pay cycle are accurate. Fake employers waste everyone's time.

## Repay from salary immediately

When M-Pesa salary hits, repay before casual spending. One hour delay becomes one week delay quickly.
