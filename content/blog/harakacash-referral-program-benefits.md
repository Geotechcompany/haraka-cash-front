Referrals reward you when friends join with your link and create an account. It is a limit tool, not free cash.

## The reward

Each qualified signup adds KES 1,000 to your available credit, up to ten people. That is up to KES 10,000 extra headroom without taking another loan.

## Where to find your link

Signed-in users see the referral page in the app. Copy the link or share the short code. Track clicks and signups there.

## Who counts as qualified

Real people who complete registration with your code. Fake numbers and self-referrals do not last. Fraud checks remove gaming attempts.

## Share responsibly

Tell friends what HarakaCash is: one-month loans and salary advances to M-Pesa with fees shown upfront. Do not promise approvals you cannot guarantee.

## Good places to share

WhatsApp family groups for genuine recommendations, workplace networks where advances are normal, chama meetings when someone asks about digital credit.

## Bad places

Spamming comment sections, posting in groups that ban promos, lying about interest rates. That burns trust and can flag your account.

## Referrals do not replace repayment

Higher limit helps when you have a plan. It does not fix missed due dates. Repay on time so the bigger limit stays useful.
