One-month loans are simple math tied to one pay cycle. Budget for the total due, not just principal.

## Write total due on paper

Principal plus interest plus any unpaid processing if applicable. That is the number your budget must fund.

## Lock cash early

Move repayment amount to M-Pesa or sacco sub-account when salary arrives. Touch it only for repayment.

## Cut one discretionary line

Data bundle upgrade, second lunch out, impulse electronics. Pick one cut for the month. You are not becoming austere forever, just funding a promise.

## Sales-based earners

Use your best sales day of the week to reserve a slice for loan repayment. Riders: target weekend peak trips for the reserve.

## Joint household budgets

Tell whoever shares M-Pesa float that KES X is spoken for on date Y. Surprises cause late pay.

## Avoid new subscriptions mid-loan

Streaming, gym, new SACCO contribution experiments wait thirty days.

## Success metric

Due date passes with payment already sent. CRB stays clean. Limit grows.
