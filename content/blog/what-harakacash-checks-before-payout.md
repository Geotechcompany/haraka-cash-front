Accepting an offer is not the last step. Verification runs before M-Pesa sends money.

## Offer acceptance

You agree to principal, interest, processing fee, and due date. That number should not change after this point.

## Processing fee payment

You pay via M-Pesa STK or displayed pay bill flow. This unlocks bureau and operations review.

## Identity match

Your ID number, name, and phone must align. Typos here cause delays, not secret declines.

## CRB pull

Licensed review of your credit file. Active defaults or heavy recent delinquency can block payout even after a yes on affordability.

## Fraud and duplicate checks

Multiple accounts on one ID or suspicious device patterns trigger manual review. One person, one account.

## Payout

When cleared, funds move to your M-Pesa. SMS confirms amount. Save it.

## If review pauses

Support may request a clearer ID photo or employer confirmation. Respond same day when possible.

## Transparency promise

Fees and repayment totals are on the offer before you pay processing. Review is about safety and compliance, not hidden pricing.
