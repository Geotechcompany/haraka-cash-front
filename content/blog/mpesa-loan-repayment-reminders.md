Reminders fail when they fire once on a busy day. Build a short system instead.

## Three-alert pattern

1. Seven days before due date: check balance needed
2. Two days before: move cash to M-Pesa
3. Morning of due date: pay before noon

## Tie to pay day if you are salaried

If salary lands the 28th and loan is due the 30th, link reminders to salary SMS, not random calendar defaults.

## Use amount labels in M-Pesa

Send yourself a dummy KES 1 note with note text "loan repay 12k" so the number stares at you in transaction history.

## Weekly voice note

Thirty seconds every Sunday: name due date and source of cash. Sounds silly. Works for busy parents and riders.

## Autopay where available

If your lender supports standing instructions or bill pay, enable it for the minimum due. Manual top-up only if you are ahead.

## Partner accountability

Share due date with someone who will ask plainly if you miss it. Shame is cheaper than late fees.

## After paying

Mark calendar item done. Check loan status in app. Limits refresh after settlement.

Set reminders the day you accept your next loan. [Apply](/apply) only when the calendar already has a repayment slot.
