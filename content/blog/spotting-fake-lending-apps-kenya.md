Scammers follow loan demand. They build slick apps that never pay out.

## PIN request equals exit

Hang up or uninstall. No exception.

## Personal till for "registration"

Legitimate lenders use company pay bills or in-app STK with merchant names you can verify.

## Guaranteed approval ads

Nobody can guarantee CRB outcomes. Ignore Telegram channels promising KES 100,000 in five minutes.

## APK files from strangers

Install only from official website links you typed yourself or reputable stores.

## Cloned logos

Compare logo, domain spelling, and support email to the company you think you are using. harakacash.co.ke support domains should match marketing.

## Upfront "insurance fee"

Another name for stolen money. Real processing fees come after a written offer.

## Data harvesting

Excessive permissions like contacts upload for a KES 2,000 loan are spyware behavior.

## What to do if scammed

Report to police cyber unit, your telco, and the impersonated brand. Change M-Pesa PIN immediately.
