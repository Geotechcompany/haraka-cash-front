Eligibility is not luck. It is a picture built from what you submit and how you have borrowed before. You can improve that picture before you apply.

## 1. Use your real M-Pesa number

The payout number must be yours and active. SIM swap issues and dormant lines cause delays that look like declines.

## 2. Match employer details

For salary advances, enter the employer name consistently across applications. Typos trigger manual review.

## 3. Declare realistic income

Do not pad income. Underwriters compare declared cash flow to requested amount. A smaller approved loan beats a decline.

## 4. Clear small overdue balances

Check your CRB status through licensed channels. Pay or restructure tiny overdue facilities first. Fresh late marks hurt more than old settled ones.

## 5. Space out applications

Five lenders in one afternoon makes you look desperate for credit. Finish one application, get a decision, then decide next steps.

## 6. Upload clear documents when asked

Blurry payslips and cropped ID photos slow CRB review. Take photos in daylight, all four corners visible.

## 7. Repay early or on time

HarakaCash rewards clean history with higher limits. Your first loan is an audition. Treat it like one.

## Referrals help limits

Invite friends with your in-app link. Each successful signup can add KES 1,000 to your available credit up to ten people. That is limit growth without borrowing more.
