Approval feels instant. Payout follows operations steps you can predict.

## Step 1: Offer on screen

Amount, fees, due date. Decision at this stage is automated for many borrowers.

## Step 2: You accept

That is your contract moment. Screenshot the offer.

## Step 3: Processing fee

Pay through the in-app flow. No fee, no bureau pull, no payout.

## Step 4: CRB and ops review

Usually same day for clean files. Edge cases wait for document clarity.

## Step 5: M-Pesa SMS

"You have received..." message hits your registered line. Verify amount matches offer principal.

## Common delays

- Wrong or offline M-Pesa number
- Name mismatch on ID
- Outstanding fraud hold from another lender
- Network downtime on Safaricom side

## What to do if money is late

Check app status, confirm fee payment SMS, then contact support with ID and application reference. Do not send PIN to anyone.

## After money lands

Use it for the expense you named in the application. Repayment planning starts now.
